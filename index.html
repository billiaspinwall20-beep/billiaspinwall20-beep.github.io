About

Hi, I'm Billi — your partner in communication.

I'm a licensed Speech-Language Pathologist with a passion for feeding therapy and early language development. I believe every child deserves a voice, and every family deserves support.

My approach is warm, play-based, and family-centered — because the best therapy happens when kids feel safe and seen.
